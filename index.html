About Me 自己紹介

My name is Ian Heung and I am a current senior majoring in physics and minoring in computer science at Davidson College. My current research is focused on implementing machine learning models for the analysis of nuclear physics experiments data. I was born and raised in Hong Kong, and am now currently studying in the United States. I am passionate about the intersection of machine learning and physics, as well as improving AI accessibility in underrepresented domains. I love language learning, am an avid badminton player, and enjoy watching sports in my free time.
