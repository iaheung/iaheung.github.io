About Me

My name is Ian Heung. I am originally from Hong Kong, and am currently located in London, UK. I am currently a postgraduate student at University College London, pursuing a MSc in Scientific and Data Intensive Computing.

I graduated magna cum laude from Davidson College in the US with a Bachelor of Science in Physics and a minor in Computer Science. During my time at Davidson, I actively participated in machine learning research focused on the analysis and classification of nuclear physics experimental data.

In addition to my studies, I work on various projects to further develop and apply my data and machine learning skills. Please feel free to explore my projects below.
